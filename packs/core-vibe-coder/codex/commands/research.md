# /research

Investigate codebase or external context and write durable findings.

## Arguments

- `question`: topic, technology, bug area, API, dependency, or design question.

## Workflow

1. Define the research question and success criteria.
2. Search local files first.
3. Use external sources or MCPs only when available and necessary.
4. Record findings in the active plan's `research/` folder, or a dated `plan/` research folder.
5. Separate facts, inferences, risks, and open questions.

## Escalation

Delegate to `researcher` for cross-source or durable research.
